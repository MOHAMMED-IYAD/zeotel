About Us

Who We Are:

ZE-OTEL Furniture & Hospitality Solutions Co. is a premier manufacturer and supplier of high-quality, stylish furniture specifically designed for the hospitality industry. We have established ourselves as a leader in creating bespoke furniture solutions that enhance the aesthetic and functional aspects of hotels, resorts, and other accommodation facilities. We blend craftsmanship with innovation to create elegant and functional furniture that enhances the guest experience in hotels, restaurants, and resorts. ZE-OTEL quality assurance plays an active role in all the processes from each step of the production to the delivery of the products. Our aim is to deliver the products to the end user in a healthy and safe way. Mission: To provide exceptional furniture that combines luxury, durability, and functionality, helping our clients create memorable guest experiences. Vision: To be the leading provider of furniture and hospitality solutions, recognized for our quality, creativity, and commitment to customer satisfaction. References

Radisson Blu Hotel – 5-Star

Our company was honored to implement a comprehensive project to furnish and equip the five-star Radisson Blu Hotel. We designed, manufactured, and supplied all the furniture and furnishings for the rooms and suites, as well as the reception halls, restaurants, and other facilities, according to the highest standards of quality and luxury. In this project, we combined modern elegance with practical comfort, reflecting the hotel's upscale identity and providing an exceptional guest experience. This achievement is a new step in our company's proven track record of executing major hotel projects both inside and outside the region.

Meet Moot

Our company was honored to fully implement the Meet Moot Restaurant project, designing and supplying all furniture and furnishings, as well as chairs, tables, and interior décor. Our role was not limited to supply alone; we also developed the restaurant's own designs, in keeping with its vision and identity. We created an upscale atmosphere that combines modern taste with practical comfort, providing visitors with a unique and distinctive experience. This project represents one of our most notable achievements in the field of restaurant furnishing and interior design, and it confirms our ability to provide comprehensive solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and creativity.
